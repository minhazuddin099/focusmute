## 📢 What is focusmute?

focusmute lets you mute and unmute your Focusrite Scarlett 4th Gen audio interface with a simple hotkey. This tool runs quietly in the background and shows your mute state right on your computer. It works well on Windows and is made to help you control your audio quickly, especially during calls or recording sessions.

You don’t need technical skills or extra setup. Just press a key and your microphone goes silent or wakes up instantly. focusmute supports the Scarlett 4th Gen models and uses the USB connection to toggle the mute.

## ⚙️ Troubleshooting

- **focusmute doesn’t detect Scarlett device**  
  Make sure your audio interface is properly connected and recognized by Windows. Check the USB cable or port if needed.

- **Mute hotkey does nothing**  
  Check if focusmute is running in your system tray. Make sure the hotkey is not conflicting with other applications.

- **focusmute icon is missing**  
  The app might be minimized. Look for it in the hidden icons area by clicking the upward arrow in the system tray.

- **Audio issues after muting**  
  Try restarting focusmute and your audio interface. Ensure your audio settings match your usage.

## 📋 How focusmute Works

focusmute uses USB HID (Human Interface Device) commands to interact with the Scarlett interface. When you press the hotkey, focusmute sends a signal to mute or unmute the microphone. The interface’s LED also changes to show the mute state. The app keeps this synced so your audio input is controlled instantly without delay.
